Data Protection Overview

We appreciate your visit to our website and your interest in our products. Protecting your personal data is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, and safeguard your personal information.

Important: Our Privacy Statement applies exclusively to our websites and services. It does not cover activities on social networks or third-party websites accessible through links on our platforms. Please review the privacy policies of those respective providers.

Collection and Processing of Personal Data

Automatic Data Collection

When you visit our websites, we automatically collect and store certain technical information:

Browser & System Information

Browser type and operating system details

Visit Analytics

Date, time, and duration of your visit

Interaction Data

Pages accessed, features used, and search queries

Network Information

IP address and Internet service provider (stored for 7 days for security)

Voluntary Data Provision

We collect additional personal data only when you voluntarily provide it through:

Registration forms and user accounts
Contact forms and customer inquiries
Live chat interactions
Surveys and feedback submissions

Please Note: Providing personal information is neither legally nor contractually required. However, certain website features may require personal data to function properly. Without this information, some features may be unavailable or have limited functionality.
