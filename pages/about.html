Our Story

Strada Auto was born from the shared vision of five passionate car enthusiasts who believed that luxury should be more than a label — it should be an experience. With backgrounds in automotive design, engineering, business, and customer service, our founders came together in 2023 with a single mission: to redefine what a high-end dealership could be in Egypt. They noticed a gap in the market — not just for premium vehicles, but for a premium service. Strada Auto was built on that foundation: not only offering world-class cars, but also offering a world-class journey from the moment a customer walks in. Whether you're looking for the latest performance machine or a timeless luxury classic, every detail matters — and at Strada Auto, we make sure it's perfect. Today, Strada Auto is more than a dealership. It's a community of dreamers, drivers, and design lovers who expect nothing less than excellence.

Our Mission

At Strada Auto, our mission is to deliver more than just premium cars — we deliver a premium experience. We aim to redefine automotive luxury in Egypt by offering a carefully curated collection of high-end vehicles paired with exceptional customer service. Every detail, from our sleek showroom design to our seamless online quote system, is tailored to meet the expectations of clients who value style, performance, and service. We’re here to make the process of owning a luxury vehicle as enjoyable and sophisticated as the car itself.

Our Vision

We envision Strada Auto as the leading name in luxury car dealerships across the region — a brand recognized not only for exclusive vehicles, but also for innovation, trust, and excellence. Our goal is to set new standards in automotive retail, blending technology with human touch. As we grow, we see Strada Auto becoming a destination for car lovers and collectors, and a platform that continues to evolve with the future of mobility.
